Barbara Ann Ray is a charming mother of one who is currently thriving in the lush, green, Pacific Northwest. She currently lives in Kirkland, Washington (although she has lived in California for a time in the past) along with her two cats—a girl tabby named “Tinkerbell” and a male black cat named “Boo”—husband, and dog—a black labrador retriever named “Jazzi.”

In her free time, she very much enjoys hobbies like gardening, birdwatching, and playing Pokemon Go. The weather in the greater Seattle area is perfect for gardening despite the ongoing rain that loves to plague the area. As such, she is very knowledgeable about flowers and loves to identify various types she sees while out and about or while on vacation. She also loves to birdwatch and recently received a birdhouse that has a camera inside of it for Christmas so that she can view the various birds that inhabit the area. Pokemon Go has been a hobby of hers since the game was released in 2017, and she can often be found taking over gyms in the game as she hikes one of the many trails or hiking paths that are available in the greater Seattle area.

For her day-to-day job, Barbara inspects medical devices—an industry that she has been in for the entirety of her career. She is excellent at what she does and works hard so that she can play harder. Her favorite vacation spots are Hawaii—where she can enjoy the warm weather—and Orlando—where her daughter currently lives and works at Walt Disney World.
