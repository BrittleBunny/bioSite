Hiking

My mother loves to hike and can often be found hiking the trail every single day! She often brings along our family dog—a black lab named Jazzi—and hits the trail, loving the time she has to enjoy nature's beauty and get her body moving. Often, she will take images of the fun scenes she finds, one of her favorite being a tree cracked from the insane heat that we have been getting in Washington state lately! Seattle is really a beautiful place, and my mother does not take advantage of that fact.

Gardening

Another thing that my mother loves is gardening. From a young age, I remember her being out the garden when the weather is nice to take care of the many flowers and vegetables that she grows there. Although she grows things such as tomatos, carrots, snap dragons, hyacinths, and (my favorite) lilacs; my mother is especially fond of roses and has a lot of different common and rare types of roses in the flower garden that boarders the entirety of our front yard!

Pokemon GO!

I do not think anyone in our family could have predicted the love that my mother would grow for a humble little mobile game such as Pokemon GO! when it released in 2017. She often compouds her love of hiking with her love of Pokemon GO!, since it is a game that one has to walk around while playing to play to its fullest extent! She will often be "taking gyms" while hiking with my dad and I, and is honestly very good at the game! She sometimes likes to drive out to raids too or tries to find raids within our immediate area.
